# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is AGROTM?
AGROTM is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform specifically designed for the agriculture sector. It combines blockchain technology with agricultural finance to provide innovative solutions for farmers, investors, and agricultural businesses.

### What makes AGROTM unique?
AGROTM stands out by:
- **Agriculture Focus**: Specifically designed for agricultural finance and tokenization
- **Multi-Chain Support**: Works on both Solana and Ethereum blockchains
- **Real-World Assets**: Connects digital tokens to real agricultural assets
- **Sustainability**: Promotes sustainable farming practices through DeFi incentives
- **Accessibility**: User-friendly interface for both crypto and non-crypto users

### Is AGROTM open source?
Yes, AGROTM is open source and available under the MIT License. You can contribute to the project through our GitHub repository.

## Technical Questions

### What blockchains does AGROTM support?
AGROTM currently supports:
- **Solana**: For high-speed, low-cost transactions
- **Ethereum**: For broader DeFi ecosystem integration

### What wallets are supported?
- **Solana**: Phantom, Solflare, Slope, Backpack
- **Ethereum**: MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet
- **Mobile**: Trust Wallet, Rainbow, Argent

### How do I connect my wallet?
1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button
2. Choose your preferred wallet
3. Approve the connection in your wallet
4. Your wallet address will be displayed

### What are the gas fees?
- **Solana**: Very low fees (~$0.00025 per transaction)
- **Ethereum**: Variable fees based on network congestion

## Staking & Farming

### What is staking?
Staking involves locking up your AGROTM tokens to earn rewards and participate in governance. Stakers receive a share of platform fees and can vote on proposals.

### How do I start staking?
1. Connect your wallet
2. Navigate to the Staking section
3. Choose a staking pool
4. Enter the amount you want to stake
5. Confirm the transaction

### What is yield farming?
Yield farming allows you to earn rewards by providing liquidity to trading pairs or participating in farming pools. You can earn both AGROTM tokens and other rewards.

### What are the APY rates?
APY rates vary based on:
- Total value locked in pools
- Market conditions
- Platform usage
- Current rates are displayed in real-time on the dashboard

### Is there a lock-up period?
Some staking pools have lock-up periods, while others allow immediate withdrawal. Check the specific pool details before staking.

## NFTs & Tokenization

### What are agricultural NFTs?
Agricultural NFTs represent real-world agricultural assets like:
- Farm land
- Crop yields
- Equipment
- Livestock
- Carbon credits

### How do I mint an NFT?
1. Navigate to the NFT section
2. Click "Mint NFT"
3. Upload your asset data
4. Set metadata and attributes
5. Pay the minting fee
6. Confirm the transaction

### Can I sell my agricultural NFTs?
Yes, you can list your NFTs on the AGROTM marketplace. Set your price and wait for buyers, or accept offers from interested parties.

### How is NFT value determined?
NFT value is based on:
- Real-world asset value
- Rarity and uniqueness
- Market demand
- Historical performance
- Agricultural data

## Security

### Is AGROTM secure?
AGROTM implements multiple security measures:
- Smart contract audits by leading firms
- Multi-signature wallets for treasury
- Insurance coverage for major pools
- Regular security assessments
- Bug bounty program

### What happens if I lose my wallet?
If you lose access to your wallet:
- **Private Keys**: Cannot be recovered - keep backups secure
- **Hardware Wallets**: Use recovery phrases to restore
- **Custodial Wallets**: Contact the wallet provider

### Are my funds insured?
Some pools have insurance coverage, but not all. Check individual pool details for insurance information.

## Governance

### How does governance work?
AGROTM uses a decentralized governance system:
- AGROTM token holders can vote on proposals
- Voting power is proportional to token holdings
- Proposals require quorum to pass
- Executed proposals automatically update the protocol

### How do I participate in governance?
1. Hold AGROTM tokens
2. Navigate to the Governance section
3. Review active proposals
4. Vote using your tokens
5. Proposals with majority support are executed

### What types of proposals can be made?
- Protocol parameter changes
- New feature additions
- Treasury allocations
- Security updates
- Community initiatives

## Performance & Scalability

### How fast are transactions?
- **Solana**: ~65,000 TPS, ~400ms finality

### Can AGROTM handle high demand?
Yes, AGROTM is designed to scale:

- Processing times may slow

- Emergency procedures are in place
